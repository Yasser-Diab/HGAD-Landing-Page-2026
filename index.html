Who We Are

HGAD (El Handasia Group for Architectural Designs) is an Egyptian leader in high-performance aluminium and glass façade systems. With over 10 years of engineering excellence and more than 100 successful projects, we combine innovation, precision, and international standards to deliver elegant yet resilient architectural solutions.

Why HGAD

  • Engineering Excellence

    Comprehensive solutions that blend technical accuracy with modern architectural aesthetics.

  • International Standards

    Our systems meet global quality benchmarks for performance, durability, and insulation.

  • Customization & Flexibility

    Tailored products designed to meet project-specific requirements, from aesthetics to engineering performance.

  • End-to-End Support

    From initial design to project delivery and after-sales assistance, HGAD stands by every customer.

Our Core Products & Systems

  • Glass Façade with Cladding

    Elegant, high-performance façades that enhance building aesthetics while providing structural reliability.

  • Curtain Wall Systems

    Lightweight yet strong aluminium and glass curtain walls engineered for safety, insulation, and contemporary design.

  • Louvre Systems

    Functional shading and airflow systems with sleek architectural expression.

  • Glass Stair Railing Systems

    Modern and transparent railing solutions for interiors and exteriors.

  • Shutter & Rolling Systems

    Durable, precisely engineered shutter systems for security and weather protection.

  • Doors & Window Systems

    High-quality aluminium doors and windows combining strength, insulation, and design flexibility.
